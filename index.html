What is JERT?

A utility token for real infrastructure

JERT supports the Cryogas ecosystem: LNG cold energy recovery terminals, containerized cold storage, LCNG fueling and B2B logistics hubs. Token utility is linked to measurable terminal services and energy flows.

  • Issued by Cryogas Kazakhstan (utility purpose)
  • Integrated with Cryogas Cold Energy Terminals
  • Designed for legal circulation through AIFC-compliant digital asset model
Vision

From Aktobe to Eurasia

The first terminal is deployed in Aktobe as a strategic hub of the Middle Corridor. The network scales to other Kazakhstan regions, the Caspian route, Caucasus gateways, and EU distribution nodes.

Token Utility

JERT is used inside Cryogas terminals and partner services as a digital access and settlement layer.

Terminal Access

Holding JERT unlocks service tiers for cold storage booking, LNG cold energy usage, and B2B logistics integration.

Service Discounts

Tiered JERT holdings provide preferential tariffs for storage, freezing, LCNG fueling and multimodal handling.

Partner Payments

JERT can be used for partial settlements with suppliers, contractors and service partners within approved legal frameworks.

2-Level Access Model

The token enables a two-tier participation model aligned with Cryogas terminal economics.

Level 1

Standard Utility Access

  • Access to terminal services by holding JERT
  • Booking cold storage capacity and processing slots
  • Digital account & service wallet inside Cryogas ecosystem
Level 2

Free Terminal Usage by Code

  • Users can receive a terminal access code through campaigns or partners
  • Code grants limited free services (e.g., storage time, handling windows)
  • Creates onboarding funnel into full utility ecosystem

Infrastructure Linked to JERT

JERT value is tied to real assets and operations, not abstract promises.

Cold Energy Terminals

LNG cold energy recovery applied to freezing and storage modules, enabling low-carbon cold chain capacity.

LCNG Stations

Integrated LNG/CNG fueling with high-pressure regasification and terminal logistics backbone.

B2B Logistics Hub

Container yards, rail sidings, reefer service zones, and multimodal Middle Corridor flows.

API Layer

Digital Infrastructure

JERT ecosystem uses an API layer to connect terminals, wallets, service bookings, and partner integrations with transparent usage accounting.

  • Terminal booking & tariff API
  • On-chain/off-chain service reconciliation
  • Partner service endpoints
VPS Nodes

5 VPS Validator/Service Nodes

The system is supported by a distributed VPS-node architecture ensuring availability, auditability, and redundancy for utility services.

  • 5 independent VPS nodes
  • Role separation: accounting, compliance, booking, partner gateway, backup
  • Failover & monitoring across regions
